HARRY POTTER'S WORLD

This project was produced in group of two. It is a website about the lore of Harry Potter. It has been develooped in native HTLM, CSS and Javascript. Actually, this website is connected to an online API gathering the most famous character of the film. Thus at the entrance, the visitor can choose one of the four family by clicking on it. Then, the creator of the chosen familly is presented in another page. Finally if the visitor decides to see the students, the students appear dynamically in the page. Also, it is possible to sort the students thanks to a form which appears by clicking on the "+" icon.
