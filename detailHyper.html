HYPER-ANTHROPOS SIMULATION

This project realized in group of two is a contemplative video game dealing with the pollution of humans. It has been created with Unity and C#. We decided to deal with 3 types of pollution : light pollution, sound pollution, and atmospheric pollution. Each type of pollution has a dedicated room were the player have to found the exit. Each of theses rooms is linked to a black box where the player can find informations about the of pollution.
