GRAPHIC DESIGN

WOMEN'S FOOTBALL WORD CUP

Theses 3 posters serie is about the FIFA woman world cup of soccer which took place in France in 2019. For the creation of these posters I was inspired by the dynamism and the strenght of the russian constructivism. Regarding the colors, I draw my inspiration from the official poster of the event. Also, this subject was really interesting for me since it deals with feminine sport. In my opinion woman soccer is not enough watch, thus I though theses posters as a way to give more visibility to this sport.
