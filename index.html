WELCOME IN MY PORTFOLIO

Welcome to my portfolio, I am Sully-Ann Sorimoutou. I am currently completing an engineering degree in IMAC at the ESIPE. I am looking for an end-of-study internship which is an opportunity for me to work in an area that interests me. In fact, for this last year of study, I chose to specialize in web development to become a full-stack developer.

FRONT END

I am used to working with several languages such as HTML 5, CSS, and Javascript. It is thanks to a few projects and experiences that I learned several javascript frameworks such as Vue, Nuxt, React, Next, or Node. I had the opportunity to create web apps for school projects or internships. Indeed, I worked for 4 months in a Hungarian company in Budapest where I mainly had to deal with the migration of the application VueStoreFront 1 to VueStoreFront 2.

BACK END

I also feel at ease with back-end development. It is thanks to many projects that I learned Php, and SQL. But I prefer working with the most recent technologies such as Node.js or Express. Also, I had the opportunity to discover through projects Firebase and the Firestore. Indeed I am currently working on a project using Firebase authentification and Firestore as a database. Finally, I can work with Docker container architecture. As a matter of fact, during my previous internship, I had to manage some Docker containers.
