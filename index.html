In fact, for this last year of study I chose to specialize in web development in order to become a full stack developer.

FRONT END

I am used to work with several languages such as HTML 5, CSS and Javascript. As a matter of fact, it is thanks to a few projects and experiences that I learn several javascript frameworks such as Vue, Nuxt, React, Next, or Node.js I had the opportunity to create several websites for school projects or internships. BACK END

I also feel at ease with back end development. In fact, it is thanks to many project that I learnt Php, and SQL . But I prefer working with most recent technologies such as Node.js or Express .
